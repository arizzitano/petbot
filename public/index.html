PETBOT v0.0.1

Call into VSee first so you can see where you're going. Then control PETBOT by pressing your arrow keys:

  • ↑ - Forward
  • ↓ - Backward
  • ← - Steer left
  • → - Steer right
  • Esc - KILLSWITCH

Bug? Strange behavior? PETBOT disobeying Asimov's laws of robotics? Shoot a quick email to arizzitano@appneta.com or ping @ari on HipChat. Better yet, open a pull request.
